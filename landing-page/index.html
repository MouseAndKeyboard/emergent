What is Emergent Civilization?

A persistent, player‑driven world where 20 – 80 people co‑operate, compete, and self‑organise into sprawling nations. No resets, no rulebooks – the society you build is the society you live in.

One life = One chance

Death is permanent. Strategy, diplomacy, and foresight keep you alive.

Hard‑coded fairness

Serverside mods guarantee no pay‑to‑win advantages and transparent governance.

Live world map & telemetry

Follow economic hubs, migration waves and conflicts in real‑time on our public map.

How a “Civilization Event” Works

  1. Wait‑list fills → we schedule the start date.
  2. Players join Discord for **whitelist** verification & briefing.

  4. Self‑government begins. Your story writes itself.
